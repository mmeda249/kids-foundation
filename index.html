Our Work

The Kids Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to serving the needs of children throughout Africa, Asia and South America. We partner with local community leaders, schools, hospitals, donors and volunteers to tackle a range of children's issues including hunger, healthcare, and education.

Natural disasters, economic instability, poor infrastructure and limited resources are just some of the issues affecting children across the globe. We work to provide food, shelter, and access to healthcare and educational resources.

Help us make the world a better place by supporting our initiatives.

Volunteer

Support from volunteers is crucial to help deliver vital resources and services to the most vulnerable members of our society. Your participation can help guarantee that a child receives meals that day, access to clean water, and reliable shelter.

Our volunteers come from a variety of backgrounds and include college students, teachers, nurses, doctors, chefs, administrative coordinators and more.
