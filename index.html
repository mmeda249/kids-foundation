What We Do:
A Global Collaborative Effort

The Kids Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to serving the needs of children throughout Africa, Asia, and Central America. We partner with local community leaders, schools, hospitals, donors and volunteers to tackle a range of children's issues including hunger, healthcare, and education.

Natural disasters, economic instability, poor infrastructure and limited resources are just some of the issues affecting children across the globe. We work to ensure children are provided with food, educational resources, and access to healthcare.
